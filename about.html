About Anatomica

Integrating science, technology, and interactive design to create a smarter learning experience.

Our Platform

Anatomica is an interactive web-based learning platform designed to help students, educators, and enthusiasts explore and understand human anatomy in a clear and engaging way. We present detailed anatomical structures through visually rich illustrations and organized content.

The Experience

By simplifying complex biological concepts, Anatomica makes learning about the human body more accessible, structured, and effective. Navigate system by system, view detailed explanations, and interact with dynamic models to enhance your understanding.

Under the Hood

We built Anatomica using modern web standards and design tools to ensure a seamless, educational journey across all devices.

HTML5 / CSS3 JavaScript Three.js / WebGL Blender (3D) Firebase DB
